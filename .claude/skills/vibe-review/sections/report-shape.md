## Report Shape

1. `## Inputs loaded`
2. `## Findings (severity desc)`
3. `## Suggested next-sprint scope`
4. `## Links`

The prior-review parser accepts both exact `## Findings` and parenthesized
`## Findings (...)`. Keep the heading text at level 2 and do not rename it.

If there are no findings, write `none` under `## Findings` and include one or
two residual-risk lines.
